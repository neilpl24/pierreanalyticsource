About This Website

PierreAnalytics is a website that provides a variety of advanced NHL statistics, presented in a similar format to tools used by analytically driven major league front offices. It was created by Neil Pierre-Louis as a personal project, and is maintained by Neil and Bennett Summy.

We are major advocates of the value of personal projects. If you'd like to build your skills working on this site, let us know!

Data

All data is sourced from the NHL's API. Unofficial API docs can be found here . All advanced stat calculations are performed by Neil and uploaded to a SQL database that this website pulls from. This website is an Angular frontend with an Express JS backend that pulls from a SQLite3 database. All player card statistics are calculated as a rate (per/60) and are percentile based.

Glossary

Name Description
xG The amount of expected goals for that a skater is on the ice for.
xGA The amount of expected goals against that a skater is on the ice for.
Finishing Calculated as what is usually referred to as "Goals Scored Above Expected," which is simply the amount of goals a skater has scored subtracted by the amount of expected goals they have generated through shots themselves.
Primary Assists Assists where the skater was the last one on the scorer's team to touch or pass the puck before the scorer obtains possession (assuming no possession loss before this) and scores.
EV Even Strength (5v5, 4v4, 3v3)
PP Powerplay (Skater advantage due to penalty, 5v4 for example)
SH Shorthanded (Skater disadvantage due to penalty, 4v5 for example)
TOI Time On Ice
